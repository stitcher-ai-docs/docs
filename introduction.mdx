StitcherAI is a **system of intelligence for IT Finance**. It builds a complete, business-aligned model of your technology spend — across cloud, SaaS, AI, and internal IT — on open standards and your own data, and makes that intelligence available to the people _and_ the AI agents who make spending decisions, at the moment they make them.

Instead of reporting on spend after the fact in a portal, StitcherAI owns a trustworthy cost model and delivers its intelligence into the workflows where technology-spend decisions actually happen.

## Two engines on one data foundation

StitcherAI is made up of two engines that share one data foundation — your cost model.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Semantic Engine" icon="diagram-project">
    Builds and maintains the cost model on a schedule (typically daily): it connects to your data, normalizes it to a common open standard, attributes and allocates cost, produces the datasets, KPIs, and forecasts you run on, and applies cost-governance controls.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Reasoning Engine" icon="robot">
    An always-present layer of IT-Finance agents that reason over the model inside your existing human and AI workflows, so cost and business context is available wherever a decision is made.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The foundation they share is the cost model itself: complete, normalized to [FOCUS](https://focus.finops.org) (the open cost and usage standard), and owned by you.

## What you get

One trustworthy, business-aligned view of _all_ technology spend — owned by you, on open formats and open standards — that both humans and AI agents can act on.

<Note>
  StitcherAI is not a dashboard, a BI tool, a single-cloud cost explorer, or a tagging product. It is the IT-Finance data-and-intelligence layer those tools sit on top of.
</Note>
