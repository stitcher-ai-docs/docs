Teams use StitcherAI wherever a technology-spend decision needs to be grounded in complete, business-aligned cost — and, increasingly, wherever an AI agent makes or informs that decision.

  <Card title="One view of all technology spend" icon="layer-group">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Cost intelligence in AI workflows" icon="robot" href="/architecture-two-engines">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Unit economics & ROI for AI" icon="chart-line">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Automate manual IT-Finance work" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
